Why Partner with Us

Building the On-Screen Sync White-Label Business: Empowering Entrepreneurs and Existing Businesses with Digital Signage Solutions.

Market Overview


Current State of the Digital Signage Industry The digital signage industry is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing demand for dynamic content delivery and enhanced customer engagement across various sectors. As of 2024:

  • Market Size: The global digital signage market is valued at approximately £25 billion and is expected to continue expanding rapidly.
  • Growth Rate: The market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of around 7-10% over the next several years.
  • Key Drivers: Factors fueling this growth include advancements in display technologies, the increasing adoption of digital signage in retail and hospitality sectors, and the rising demand for interactive and personalized content.
Future Potential
  • Technological Advancements: Innovations in display technology, such as OLED and 4K/8K screens, are driving market expansion by enabling more engaging and high-quality content.
  • Increased Adoption: There is a growing trend toward integrating digital signage with other technologies like IoT and AI, which enhances functionality and provides deeper analytics.
  • Broadening Applications: Digital signage is increasingly being used in new sectors, including healthcare, education, and corporate environments, expanding market opportunities.
On-Screen Sync Business Model Overview

The On-Screen Sync white-label solution provides a fully branded digital signage service for a low monthly fee of £19.99. Partners receive:

  • A Cloud-Based CMS: Manage digital signage content and operations under your own brand with our powerful, user-friendly Content Management System.
  • White-Label Website: A customizable website where your clients can log in, manage their content, and access analytics, all branded with your company’s name.
  • Hardware Integration: Access to pre-approved digital signage hardware or the option to use your own.
  • Ongoing Support: Comprehensive training, technical support, and marketing resources to help you succeed.
Revenue Streams for On-Screen Sync Partners
  • Initial Setup/License Fee: Charge clients for setting up their digital signage networks, including hardware installation and configuration.
  • Recurring Platform Fees: Collect the monthly fee from clients for access to your branded digital signage platform. Your monthly cost is just £19.99, allowing you to set your own pricing for clients and create a profit margin.
  • Hardware Sales: Generate additional income by selling or renting digital signage hardware to clients.
  • Content Creation Services: Offer services to create and manage content for your clients, either as part of a package or as an additional fee.
  • Advertising Revenue: Monetize high-traffic screens by offering local advertising opportunities, creating another potential revenue stream.
